Welcome to our visualization tool for Tiktok usage amongst French media

About us

Welcome to our website, where we offer 5 different data visualizations centered around French speaking media. This website was made in the context of the course Data Visualization (INF8808E) at Polytechnique Montréal. The original data was provided by Jean-Hugues Roy, from UQAM. Whether you're an industry professional, a journalist, a researcher, or simply someone interested in the world of French media, our website will provide you interesting information.

This website was made by Maximus Shymko, Zachary Thibodeau, Mohammed Aymen Chebbi, Thierry Ndimis, Augustin Lompo, Murhad Masabbir, Théo Reibell and Yannick Désiré

Explore the data

As stated earlier, there are 5 different data visualizations offered in this website, which are

  • Media outlets :

    this visualization shows the evolution of viewer engagement for different media outlets

  • Hashtags :

    this visualization shows the top 10 hashtags that generate the most viewer engagement

  • Video length :

    this visualization shows what video length generates the most and the least amount of viewer engagement

  • Songs :

    this visualization shows which songs generate the most and the least amount of viewer angagement

  • Video postings :

    this visualization shows what time of day and what day of week seem to generate the most amount of viewer engagement

Have fun exploring the data!
